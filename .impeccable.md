# Design Context — Kana Smash

## Target audience

Beginner Japanese learners (English speakers) practising hiragana and katakana
recognition, primarily on their own phone in short sessions (commute, breaks).
Clarity and accessibility are hard requirements from the creator.

## Use cases

- Pick a script (hiragana / katakana) and drill a small group of characters
  via multiple-choice questions (kana→romaji, romaji→kana, listen→kana).
- Review a heatmap of weak characters and run focused weak-area sessions.
- Installable PWA, works offline, progress stored locally on device.

## Brand personality / tone

Warm, calm, encouraging — Japanese stationery / washi-paper study notebook,
not a neon gamified toy. Culturally authentic touches: 〇 (maru) for correct,
✗ (batsu) for incorrect, hanko-stamp-style branding.

## Visual direction

- Warm cream washi paper background; sumi-ink (warm near-black) text.
- Primary action colour: ai-iro indigo. Accent: shu-iro vermillion.
  Success: matcha green. All AA-contrast on the paper background.
- Kana rendered large in the system Japanese stack (Hiragino / Noto Sans JP).
- Mobile-first, generous tap targets (≥48px), visible focus rings,
  `prefers-reduced-motion` respected, aria-live feedback in quizzes.
